# Participant User Guide

Welcome to the Metabolic-Tracker! This guide will help you get started with tracking your health data and connecting with your coach.

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Go to the app URL provided by your administrator
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click **Sign In**

**First Time Login?**
- Use the temporary password provided in your welcome email
- You'll be prompted to create a new password on first login
- Choose a strong password (at least 8 characters with letters and numbers)

### Forgot Your Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link (check spam folder if not found)
4. Click the link and create a new password

**Still having trouble?** Contact your coach or administrator for help.

---

## Entering Your Health Data

### Logging Glucose Readings

1. From the dashboard, click **+ Add Metric** or the glucose icon
2. Select **Glucose** as the metric type
3. Enter your reading value
4. Select the timing (fasting, before meal, after meal)
5. Add any notes if needed
6. Click **Save**

**Tips:**
- Log readings as soon as possible after taking them
- Include context in notes (e.g., "felt dizzy", "after exercise")
- Be consistent with when you take readings

### Logging Blood Pressure

1. Click **+ Add Metric**
2. Select **Blood Pressure**
3. Enter systolic (top number) and diastolic (bottom number)
4. Add notes if needed
5. Click **Save**

### Logging Weight

1. Click **+ Add Metric**
2. Select **Weight**
3. Enter your weight
4. For best tracking, weigh yourself at the same time each day
5. Click **Save**

### Logging Waist Measurement

1. Click **+ Add Metric**
2. Select **Waist**
3. Measure at your natural waist (usually at belly button level)
4. Enter the measurement
5. Click **Save**

## Tracking Your Meals

### Logging Food (Text Entry)

1. Click **+ Add Food** or tap the food icon
2. Type a description of what you ate (e.g., "grilled chicken salad with vinaigrette")
3. Select the meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack)
4. The app will automatically estimate nutrition information
5. Review and adjust if needed
6. Click **Save**

### Logging Food (Photo Entry)

1. Click **+ Add Food**
2. Click **Take Photo** or **Upload Photo**
3. Capture or select a photo of your meal
4. The app will analyze the image and estimate contents
5. Review the detected foods and make corrections if needed
6. Click **Save**

**Tips for better photo analysis:**
- Take photos in good lighting
- Include the whole plate in the frame
- Take photos before you start eating

### Viewing Your Food Log

1. Go to **Food Log** from the menu
2. See all your entries organized by date
3. Click any entry to view details or make edits
4. Filter by meal type or date range

## Viewing Your Progress

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- Recent metrics at a glance
- Trends for the past week
- Messages from your coach
- Quick action buttons

### Viewing Charts and Trends

1. Go to **Trends** from the menu
2. Select the metric you want to view (Glucose, Weight, etc.)
3. Choose a time range (week, month, 3 months)
4. View your progress chart
5. Hover/tap on points for details

**Understanding Your Charts:**
- Green zones indicate healthy ranges
- Yellow zones indicate areas for attention
- Red zones indicate values outside healthy ranges
- Your coach can help explain your specific targets

### Viewing Nutrition Summary

1. Go to **Nutrition** from the menu
2. See your daily calorie and macro breakdown
3. Compare against your personalized targets
4. View weekly averages

## Communicating with Your Coach

### Sending Messages

1. Click **Messages** from the menu
2. Your conversation with your coach will appear
3. Type your message in the text box
4. Click **Send**

**What to message about:**
- Questions about your health data
- Concerns about symptoms or readings
- Updates on how you're feeling
- Questions about recommendations

### When to Contact Your Coach

**Message your coach for:**
- General questions about the program
- Non-urgent health concerns
- Progress updates
- Questions about logging data

**Contact your doctor immediately for:**
- Severe symptoms (chest pain, difficulty breathing)
- Very high or very low glucose readings
- Symptoms that concern you
- Any medical emergency

## Common Questions (FAQ)

### Account & Login

**Q: I can't log in. What should I do?**
A: First, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link. If that doesn't work, contact your coach or administrator.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Change Password. Enter your current password and your new password.

**Q: The app says my session expired. What does that mean?**
A: For security, you're automatically logged out after a period of inactivity. Simply log in again to continue.

### Entering Data

**Q: I forgot to log a meal/reading. Can I still enter it?**
A: Yes! When entering data, you can adjust the date and time to record past entries.

**Q: I made a mistake in an entry. How do I fix it?**
A: Go to your log, find the entry, click on it, and select "Edit". Make your changes and save.

**Q: Can I delete an entry?**
A: Yes, click on the entry and select "Delete". Note that your coach may see a record of deleted entries for safety reasons.

**Q: Why does the food analysis sometimes get things wrong?**
A: The AI does its best to identify foods, but it's not perfect. You can always edit the details to correct any mistakes.

### Charts & Progress

**Q: Why is my chart showing unexpected values?**
A: Double-check that you entered the data correctly. If an entry is wrong, edit it to fix your chart.

**Q: What do the colored zones on my chart mean?**
A: The colors indicate healthy ranges personalized for you. Your coach can explain your specific targets.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, go to Settings > Export Data to download your information.

### Messaging

**Q: How quickly will my coach respond?**
A: Coaches typically respond within 24 hours on business days. For urgent concerns, contact your healthcare provider.

**Q: Can I send photos to my coach?**
A: Currently, you can share photos through your food log entries. Your coach can see these in your profile.

## Troubleshooting

### App Not Loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page (pull down on mobile, click refresh on desktop)
3. Clear your browser cache (Settings > Clear Browsing Data)
4. Try a different browser (Chrome or Safari recommended)
5. If still not working, contact support

### Data Not Saving

1. Check your internet connection
2. Don't navigate away while saving - wait for confirmation
3. Try again in a few minutes
4. If repeated issues, note what you were trying to save and contact support

### Charts Not Displaying

1. Ensure you have data for the selected time period
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### Photo Upload Issues

1. Ensure the photo is not too large (under 10MB)
2. Supported formats: JPG, PNG
3. Try taking a new photo with better lighting
4. If repeated issues, try text entry instead
